Welcome to Pura Village

Pura is a small Village/hamlet in Kushtagi Taluk in Koppal District of Karnataka State, India. It comes under Pura Panchayath. It belongs to Gulbarga Division . It is located 64 KM towards North from District head quarters Koppal. 411 KM from State capital Bangalore

Pura is surrounded by Hungund Taluk towards North , Yelburga Taluk towards South , Lingsugur Taluk towards East , Gangavathi Taluk towards South .Lingsugur , Ron , Muddebihal , Sindhnur are the near by Cities to Pura.Pura Local Language is Kannada. Pura Village Total population is 1295 and number of houses are 209. Female Population is 50.6%. Village literacy rate is 53.2% and the Female Literacy rate is 22.5%.
